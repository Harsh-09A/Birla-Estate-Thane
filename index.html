About Birla Estates

The Birla Group, officially known as the Aditya Birla Group, is one of the largest and most diversified conglomerates in India, with a rich history spanning over 150 years. Founded by Seth Shiv Narayan Birla in 1857, the group has grown into a global powerhouse with a presence in over 36 countries across six continents. Under the leadership of the late Aditya Vikram Birla and now his son Kumar Mangalam Birla, the group has evolved into a market leader, known for its commitment to innovation, sustainability, and corporate social responsibility.

Birla Estates, the real estate arm of the Birla Group, has made significant strides in the Indian real estate market. Known for delivering high-quality residential and commercial properties, the company focuses on creating spaces that blend modernity with comfort, offering world-class amenities and superior infrastructure. Birla Estates is committed to excellence in design and customer satisfaction, with several successful developments across major cities. The group’s legacy of integrity, reliability, and excellence continues to shape its real estate ventures, making it a trusted name in the industry.
